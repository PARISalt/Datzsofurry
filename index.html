Terms of Service

Age Requirement: You must be 18 years or older to commission a fursuit, or have explicit permission and guidance from a parent or legal guardian.

Payments & Deposits: A 30% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your spot in the queue and purchase materials. Payment plans are available and can be discussed during the quoting process.

Cancellations: Commissions can be canceled before material orders are placed for a partial refund (minus the deposit). Once fur has been cut, no refunds will be issued.

Deadlines: Completion times vary based on the complexity of the suit and current queue. Estimated completion dates will be provided but are subject to change.
